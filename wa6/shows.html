Privacy Statement
At Boulder Musician’s Bulletin, we respect your privacy and want you to understand how we handle your information.

What we collect: If you fill out our band submission form, we collect the name and text you type into the form fields. We also save your theme preference (light or dark mode).

Why we collect it: Saving your form entries helps you keep your progress if you leave the page or come back later. Saving your theme choice makes sure the site looks the way you prefer each time you visit.

How long we store it and where: This information is only stored locally in your web browser. This means it never leaves your device or gets sent to us. Data stays in your browser until you clear it yourself.

How you can control data collection: You can use the “Clear Local Data” button at any time to erase your saved form input and theme settings. You may also clear your browser’s local storage manually through your browser settings. We don’t collect any personal data on our end, and no data is shared with third parties.
